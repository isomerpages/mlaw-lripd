<p>
<br><strong>The Licensing Officer</strong>
<br>
<br>The Licensing Officer is a public officer appointed by the Minister of
Law to oversee the licensing and regulaton of Insolvency Practitioners
(“<strong>IPs</strong>”) in Singapore. The Licensing Officer heads the
Licensing &amp; Regulation of Insolvency Practitioner Division to manage
and regulate these licensees. The division also contributes towards making
Singapore a lead hub for insolvency and debt-restructuring work by promoting
and maintaining a trusted and professional environment for professionals
engaging in insolvency and restructuring practice.
<br>
</p>
<p>
<br><strong>Our role</strong> 
<br>
</p>
<p>Our functions and roles include:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Granting and renewal of IP licences</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintaining a register of licensed IPs in Singapore</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Promoting and maintaining high standards of toe IP profession</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Investigating into complaints against the conduct of IPs</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Taking appropriate enforcement actions against errant IPs
<br>
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
